Kaka-kompyuter mo ‘yan!

Hey, did you hear about the upcoming event at the CCS department of our University? They're organizing CS DAYS: A Digital Day Off, and it's going to be a blast! It's happening from April 24 to 26, and we're all invited to take a break from our boring lectures and dive into some exciting activities.

The event is packed with fun and games. We'll be having mini intramurals, friendly competitions, cultural nights, and a banging party that you wouldn't want to miss. From basketball to volleyball, we've got outdoor games for all the sports enthusiasts. And, if you're not into sports, you can show off your talent in the cultural night by performing skits, dances, songs, and others that you're best at. The three-day event is going to be lit, and I can't wait to join the party.

To spice things up, the organizers have randomly assigned everyone into six teams based on their year level, ranging from first-year to fourth-year students. It's going to be a fierce competition, and the team with the highest score will be declared the winner. I'm excited to know which team I'll be in and which team will come out as the champion. All in all, CS DAYS: A Digital Day Off is the perfect opportunity for us to take a break from our academic routine, bond with our classmates, and create memories that we'll cherish for a long time. So, mark your calendars and get ready to have some fun!
